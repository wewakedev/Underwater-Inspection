Our Underwater Inspection Services

Underwater Inspection Services are world leaders in the underwater inspection field - because that is all we do - we specialise only in underwater inspections.

Bridges

The Underwater Inspection Services team has done hundreds of examinations on the underwater elements of bridges all around the world, giving them a thorough understanding of bridge construction and degradation, as well as the special difficulties that impact railway bridges. We check (and appreciate) many diverse bridge building methods from different ages and places as part of our relationship with UNESCO doing underwater examinations on World Heritage Bridges, and then provide advice on their continuing upkeep. No other team has ever carried out underwater examinations on such a wide range of bridges throughout the world.

Wharves and jetties

Underwater Inspection Services regularly perform underwater inspections on wharves, piers and jetties including one of the most important inspections in the life of a structure - the inspection at handover from constructor to client, and recently performed this service for one of the longest loading jetties in Australia. Our team has been providing underwater inspection services for the wharves and jetties in Australia for several years, and we’ve really come to understand how fragile much of the mooring infrastructure and harbor facilities can be. We provide every client with a custom report detailing any improvements or repairs that may need to be made in order to keep your facility functioning at its best with minimal downtime.

Shipwrecks & Archaeological Sites

UnderWater inspection services are providing you the best and innovative way to inspect your objects under water. Whether it is a Shipwreck or any other Underwater objects, UWIS has the team to help you with inspection. We have experience working in Australia, inspecting various shipwrecks over there. If you are in need of an inspection service, UnderWater Inspection Services (UWIS) can help you by sending our best inspectors and support staff to help you. We have always had a strong interest in marine archaeology and have participated in a number of maritime archaeological projects throughout the Mediterranean and Middle East, and are continually looking for new opportunities.

Fire Water Storage Tanks

Underwater Inspection Services perform the routine inspections required by AS1851 for many of the fire service companies in Melbourne. We do a complete inspection to make sure that everything is in proper working order. If not, we will take care of everything and make the necessary repairs. We’re the experts in underwater inspections and provide professional services related to fire water storage tank surveys. UWIS has frequent and continuing work helping industry with the routine necessity for fire water tank inspection and cleaning.
