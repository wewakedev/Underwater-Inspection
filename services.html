Our Underwater Inspection Services

Underwater Inspection Services are world leaders in the underwater inspection field - because that is all we do - we specialise only in underwater inspections.

Bridges

Having performed thousands of inspections on the underwater elements of bridges all over the world the Underwater Inspection Services team have a deep knowledge of bridge construction and deterioration, and the unique issues that affect railway bridges. Our partnership with UNESCO performing underwater inspections on World Heritage Bridges sees us inspecting (and appreciating) many different bridge construction methods from different ages and regions. and then providing advice on their ongoing maintenance. No other team has ever performed underwater inspections on such a diverse array of bridges all over the world.

Wharves and jetties

Underwater Inspection Services regularly perform underwater inspections on wharves, piers and jetties including one of the most important inspections in the life of a structure - the inspection at handover from constructor to client, and recently performed this service for one of the longest loading jetties in Australia.
